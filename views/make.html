Note: If possible page counts should conform to the following. Otherwise blank pages will be printed and must be removed. Page count should always be even. Odd page count zines will have blank pages that will not be removable.

  • quarter page zines: multiples of 8 (8, 16, 24 etc)
  • half page zines: multiples of 4 (4, 8, 12 etc)
  • full / double / triple / quadrouple page zines: multiples of 2 (2, 4, 6 etc)
